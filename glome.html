Glome

A 4D hypersphere — a stack of ordinary 3D spheres that shrink to a point at each "pole" along the 4th axis. Three rotations are ordinary and just spin the whole stack rigidly. Three don't exist in our world: they push points between shells, so the sphere bulges, swirls, and folds through itself without ever showing a seam. Click a "spin" button to set that axis rotating.
